It will tear through anybody in your way, endgame or not. It has some bullet spread and lower accuracy so shooting from a distance is a bad idea. This will do less damage the further away you get.

Grappler

Grappler is a utility weapon in Battle Royale that is available as a Epic variant. It can be fired at a nearby object to pull the player's character towards it and can be also fired as chain multiple shots to travel great distances. Grappler has 15 charges and discarded when all charges are depleted. It can be found in Vending Machines, floor loot, Treasure Chests, Supply Drops, and Supply Llamas. It was first introduced during PAX West and was released along with the High Stakes Event..
